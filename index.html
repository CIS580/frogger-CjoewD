Play Frogger

Instructions

Controls:
   Movement: up, down, right or w, s, d keys
   Restart Game: enter key
   Restart Mid Game: esc key

Rules:
   1. Cross the rivers and roads to get to the other side.
   2. Don't let the cars hit you.
   3. Make sure to use the logs to cross rivers! (Becareful, sometimes they sink to resurface later!)

There are a total of 5 levels, each of which increase the river flow speed and speed limit on the roads.

You have three lives, good luck!
